Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teasponns yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg pluss 1 yolk
  • ¼ cup butter

For The Filling:

  • ⅔ cup brown sugar
  • ½ tablespoons ground cinnemon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into the large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise it up for 2 hours
